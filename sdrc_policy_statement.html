Privacy Policy for SDRC

Effective Date: August 29, 2023

Welcome to SDRC, brought to you by the Software Development and Research Center at UET Peshawar. Our commitment to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information is unwavering. This Privacy Policy lays out our data collection procedures, usage methods, and protective measures. By engaging with our app, you are in agreement with the guidelines described in this policy.

Information We Gather

User Registration:
Upon registering with SDRC, we gather your name, email address, password, as well as optional details like gender and skills. This information is collected through Firebase Authentication and Real-Time Firebase Database simultaneously.

Course Registration:
For enrolling in courses, we compile your selected courses and associate them with the relevant user names and email addresses. This data aids communication and grants you course access.

Opportunities to Join Our Teams:
Should you express interest in joining our teams, we collate your name, email address, phone number, birthdate, LinkedIn and GitHub profile URLs, your role or position of interest, skills, past experience, and how you discovered us. This information assists in evaluating your application and facilitates subsequent communication.

Contact Us:
The "Contact Us" section offers valid email and phone contact details for use outside the app. Within the app, there's no direct access for chat or in-app emailing.

How We Utilize Your Information

User Communication:
We may employ your email address or phone number to reach out for verification, course updates, team prospects, or responses to queries. Your data remains protected and secure beyond these uses.

Course Management:
The data collected during course registration aids us in managing course involvement and keeping registered users informed about course-related matters.

Team Application Assessment:
Information gathered during the team application process supports the evaluation of applications and the subsequent selection process.

Communication and Updates:
Your provided contact details might be used to share updates regarding upcoming events, news, and pertinent information about our services.

Data Storage and Security

Firebase Storage:
User data associated with applications for various positions is stored in Firebase Storage. This data retains its intended purpose and is not shared with external parties.

Security Measures:
We implement reasonable security measures through Firebase Auth and Firebase Database to prevent unauthorized access, tampering, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

Developer Information:

Mission and Objectives:

Upcoming Events:
You can access information about upcoming events, which is sourced from the Firebase database and updated regularly.
